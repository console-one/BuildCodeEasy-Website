BuildCodeEasy Partnership Program

At BuildCodeEasy, we pioneered coding bootcamps with a vision to influence positive change. However, we soon realized that our reach within the community was not as profound as we had hoped. To bridge this gap, we introduced the BuildCodeEasy Partnership Program, offering complimentary in-person coding workshops to educational entities, non-profits, and community-driven initiatives across the GTA.

Delivered by our experienced cadre of industry experts, these workshops promise unparalleled instruction coupled with practical industry insights, all with no entry barriers. Our goal is to democratize coding knowledge, ensuring that everyone can harness the power of the digital realm and make their distinctive mark. Should your institution resonate with our mission and wish to join us on this transformative endeavor, we welcome you to connect with us via the contact form provided.

Our Story

To build a website in the cloud, there is an extensive amount of knowledge needed as Cloud IT providers continue to make products that are so complex to use correctly, that they require highly skilled customers for their consumption. Thus, both entrepreneurs and SMBs are forced to build their cloud presence through low-code no-code platforms, forfeiting both customizability and scalability. In fact, due to this issue, it is projected that 70% of all new business applications in 2025 will be built using low-code no-code platforms.

In 2023, BuildCodeEasy was established to teach individuals how to create both customizable and scalable cloud-based websites and applications, perfectly suited for today's evolving IT environments. We offer this premium training at an affordable rate, ensuring proficiency in just two weeks!
